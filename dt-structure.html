1 Data Team Structure

The mission of the Data Team is:

To provide data products and capacity to research team members and external stakeholders with integrity and in a timely fashion.

1.1 Members

The Data Team is currently a sub-member of the PELE research group. It is composed of Core and Advisory members:

Advisory members guide the Data Team in their priorities given the needs of other research teams and provide outlook support for the data team roadmap.
